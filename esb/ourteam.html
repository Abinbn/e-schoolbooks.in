VISION AND MISSION

Project Thoth aims to create a device that can carry textbooks and books without the burden of heavy weights, making studying interactive, smart, and easy.

By creating a device that can carry textbooks and books without the weight burden, studying becomes interactive, smart, and easy. These devices eliminate the need for students to purchase textbooks every year, including 1* and 2* part textbooks, as they only need to buy the device when they join school. Consequently, the government can save printing costs by not producing over 50,320,700 textbooks per year. Students can use the device for all classes by simply adding new books each year. Moreover, these devices enable students to access HD videos and images related to their study content even without an internet connection. Furthermore, the integration of VR (virtual reality) and AR (augmented reality) enhances interactivity and simplifies the learning process. This innovative solution not only reduces the burden of carrying multiple physical books but also contributes to environmental conservation by reducing tree cutting for paper production. With this device, students can access realistic virtual books offline and also connect to the internet when required, offering a multitude of opportunities and features.

Important points:

  • The government no longer needs to print books.
  • Students no longer need to carry many books.
  • Students don't need to buy textbooks every year.
  • The government can easily make changes to textbooks and more.

Our Process

Prioritizing Eco-Friendliness in Digital Tablet Design and Production

Yes, it is possible to create an eco-friendly digital tablet by considering various factors throughout its lifecycle, including the materials used, energy consumption, manufacturing processes, and end-of-life disposal. Here are some key considerations for designing an eco-friendly digital tablet:

  1. Materials: Opt for sustainable and recyclable materials in the tablet's construction, such as recycled plastics, bio-based materials, or responsibly sourced components. It is essential to avoid using hazardous substances like lead, mercury, and PVC.
  2. Energy Efficiency:Design the tablet to be energy-efficient by utilizing low-power components, optimizing software to reduce energy consumption, and implementing power-saving features like automatic screen brightness adjustment, sleep modes, and power management settings.
  3. Renewable Energy:Encourage the use of renewable energy sources, such as solar or wind power, in the tablet manufacturing process to minimize carbon emissions and decrease the environmental impact.
  4. Packaging:Minimize and use recyclable packaging materials to reduce waste. Whenever possible, opt for recycled or biodegradable packaging options.
  5. Repairability and Upgradability: Prioritize repairability by designing the tablet with easy access to components and replaceable parts. This approach extends the lifespan of the device and reduces electronic waste. Additionally, consider enabling upgradability to decrease the need for frequent device replacements.
  6. Recycling and Disposal:Establish a recycling program for the tablets at the end of their life cycle. Design the device to be easily disassembled, allowing for the proper recycling or disposal of different components.
  7. Responsible Manufacturing:Ensure that the manufacturing process adheres to environmentally friendly practices, such as waste reduction, water consumption minimization, and emissions control. Collaborate with suppliers who follow sustainable and ethical standards.
  8. Life Cycle Assessment:Conduct a life cycle assessment to identify areas for improvement and measure the environmental impact of the tablet at each stage, from raw material extraction to manufacturing, use, and disposal.

By incorporating these principles into the design and production of digital tablets, it is possible to create more eco-friendly devices that minimize environmental impact and promote sustainability. Such initiatives align with our company's commitment to contributing positively to the environment and shaping a better future for generations to come.
